🔥 Furnace

📦 Materials Needed

  • 8x Cobblestone (any stone type)

🔨 Crafting Steps

  1. Open the crafting table (3x3 grid).
  2. Fill all cells except the center with cobblestone.
  3. Leave the middle cell empty.
  4. Drag the furnace into your inventory.
  5. Place it in the world to smelt ores and cook food.
